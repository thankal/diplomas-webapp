About Us

Thanks for visiting our platform for diploma theses! This website links professors and students, offering an easy and practical way for professors to share their thesis topics and for students to apply to them. You can find motivated students to work on your thesis projects by simply posting your thesis topics online as a professor. Additionally, as a student, you can look through the available subjects, apply to the ones that interest you, and begin working on your diploma thesis. We sincerely hope you benefit from this resource and use it to fulfill your academic objectives!
